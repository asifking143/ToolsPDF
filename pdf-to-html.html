Why convert PDF to HTML?

Publishing content directly to the web requires HTML. PDFs are notoriously difficult for search engines to crawl dynamically and don't always scale well on mobile devices. By converting a PDF to HTML, you make the text reflowable, responsive, and completely accessible to standard web browsers.

Our client-side tool extracts the textual layers of your document and wraps them in structural HTML tags `p`, `div`, and `br`, producing a foundation you can easily style with CSS later.
